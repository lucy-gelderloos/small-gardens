Spotlight: Scarlet Runner Beans

If you have a southern exposure, scarlet runner beans can provide a fast-growing source of shade just in time for the heat of late summer. This plant can climb up to 10 feet and features large, bright green leaves and brilliant scarlet flowers. Provide a trellis or net to support the vines.
